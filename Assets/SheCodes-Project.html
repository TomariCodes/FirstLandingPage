I LOVE music

Especially "Run For the Hills" by Tate McRae

“Run For the Hills” is the third single of Tate McRae’s sophomore album. In the song Tate talks about having a deep desire to always be close to her lover. Similar to the theme of the album, Tate talks about how in the moment she is so deeply in love that she becomes blind to everything else in the world.
